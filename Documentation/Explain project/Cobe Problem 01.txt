The concept of Italian law but also international law, is that an author of the work may claim his paternity only when the work has been distributed. If the author of the work do not want to publish the work, then you must deposit it in a special office, in the case of Cobe571 who is Italian, was supposed to deposit the work at the SIAE ([url]https://www.siae.it/it[/url]).

Stating that the work was to him "stolen" during the realization of the work, is an affirmation worthless as it requires an objective response. For example, an explicit relationship of cooperation that was no longer there after the present work by Cobe571, as he himself wrote:
